About Our Ministry

MISSION STATEMENT

To raise and develop believers of christ that are fulfilling divine purpose for their lives and making impact on earth and enter the kingdom of God at the end.


VISSION STATEMENT

To teach believers, raise purposeful leaders and heal the sick in order to develop people that are holistically

equipped; spirit, souls and body for the fulfillment of destiny and making heaven at the end


Join us as we grow together in faith and serve our community with love and compassion.

Praise & Worship

Experience God's Presence Through Worship

At Revealed Word of God Global Ministry, we believe worship is a vital part of our relationship with God. Our praise and worship sessions are filled with the power of the Holy Spirit, creating an atmosphere where lives are transformed.

Join us every Sunday as we lift up holy hands and voices in praise to our King. Our worship team is dedicated to leading the congregation into the presence of God through contemporary and traditional worship songs.

Experience the joy of worship, the power of prayer, and the transformation that comes from encountering God in our services.
